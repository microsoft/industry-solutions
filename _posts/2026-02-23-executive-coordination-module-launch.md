We're excited to announce the **Executive Coordination** module — a starter kit for managing high-level directives, executive actions, and organizational taskers.

## What is the Executive Coordination Module?

The Executive Coordination module provides structured tracking for executive actions and directives that require coordinated response across organizational units. It enables leadership teams to issue taskers, assign accountability, track progress, and maintain visibility into action completion.

Key capabilities include:

- **Action Management** – Document executive actions, directives, and taskers with clear requirements and deadlines
- **Accountability Tracking** – Assign responsibility and track action assignee progress through completion
- **Decision Documentation** – Capture formal decisions with supporting context and approval workflows
- **Supporting Context** – Link actions to legal authorities, agreements, strategic initiatives, and risk assessments
- **Progress Visibility** – Monitor action health, status updates, and completion metrics

## What's Included

This model-driven app starter kit includes:

- **12 entities** for executive action tracking and coordination
- **Main forms and active views** for managing directives, assignees, and status tracking
- **Integration with Core module** for organizational structure, legal authorities, governance frameworks, and strategic initiatives

The module provides implementors with a baseline application for tracking high-level organizational coordination that can be customized for specific governance workflows and reporting requirements.

## Getting Started

The Executive Coordination module is available in the [FAST Modules Repository](https://github.com/microsoft/industry-apps) under the MIT license. It requires the Core module as a foundation.
