We're excited to announce the **HR Administration** module — a starter kit for comprehensive workforce position and employment action management.

## What is the HR Administration Module?

The HR Administration module provides structured data capture for managing workforce positions, employment actions, time and attendance, workplace requests, and employee compliance. It enables HR teams to maintain position structures, process personnel actions, manage leave requests, and track employee declarations and compliance requirements.

Key capabilities include:

- **Position Management** – Define position structures with job classifications, pay grades, clearance requirements, and organizational placement
- **Employment Actions** – Process hiring, promotions, transfers, assignments, and separations with approval workflows
- **Time and Attendance** – Manage time off requests, telework arrangements, and work schedule tracking
- **Workplace Requests** – Document accommodation requests, disciplinary actions, and employee grievances
- **Compliance Tracking** – Maintain employee declarations, training completions, and regulatory compliance records

## What's Included

This model-driven app starter kit includes:

- **21 entities** covering the full spectrum of HR administration
- **Main forms and active views** for position tracking, employment actions, and employee requests
- **Integration with Core module** for organizational structure, job classifications, clearance levels, and compliance frameworks

The module provides implementors with a baseline HR management application that can be customized to align with specific workforce policies, approval processes, and regulatory requirements.

## Getting Started

The HR Administration module is available in the [FAST Modules Repository](https://github.com/microsoft/industry-apps) under the MIT license. It requires the Core module as a foundation.
