## Introducing the Core Portal

We're excited to announce the release of **Core Portal v1.0.0.0**. This is our inaugural entry into the portal space, and like that first batch of holiday cookies that's slightly burned around the edges, it's a solid starting point with plenty of room for you to add your own magic.

## What's Under the Tree

The Core Portal delivers the essentials - think of it as the government equivalent of getting socks for Christmas. Not flashy, but absolutely necessary:

- **USWDS Identification Banner** - Because compliance is the gift that keeps on giving
- **Header with Search Bar** - For when you need to find something (like where you put that important document)
- **Navigation Menu Framework** - The bones are there, you just need to add the meat
- **Footer** - Every good story needs an ending
- **Responsive Design** - Works on desktop, tablet, and phone

The Core Portal provides the structural scaffolding that follows USWDS standards, so you can focus on adding the functionality your agency actually needs instead of figuring out CSS Grid layouts at 2 AM.
