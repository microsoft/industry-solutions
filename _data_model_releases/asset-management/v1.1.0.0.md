## Nov-11, 2025

This release adds new choice fields and asset tracking functionality. The updates expand the Asset entity with fields for property management, inventory tracking, and financial information.

**What's Added:**
- **Status Tracking**: Asset status choice field with lifecycle values from acquisition through disposal
- **Identification Fields**: Serial numbers, service tags, and RF tags for asset identification  
- **Financial Fields**: Unit cost and quantity fields for cost tracking
- **Assignment Fields**: Organization Unit lookup for institutional assignment
- **Location Fields**: Location lookup with notes and inventory date tracking

These fields support basic asset management workflows including inventory, financial tracking, and organizational assignment.

-   **Asset Status**
    - New choice field with values: In Transit, In Storage, Awaiting Assignment, Issued, Under Maintenance / Repair, Lost / Stolen, Retired, Pending Disposition, Disposed
    - Enables comprehensive tracking of asset lifecycle states and status transitions.

-   **Asset**
    - **Asset Status**: New choice field (global Asset Status) for tracking current asset state
    - **Organization Unit**: New lookup field to Organization Unit for organizational assignment tracking
    - **Serial Number**: New text field for unique hardware identification
    - **Service Tag**: New text field for manufacturer service identification
    - **RF Tag**: New text field for RFID-based tracking systems
    - **Location**: New lookup field to Location for physical asset placement
    - **Location Note**: New multi-line plain text field for additional location details
    - **Purchased Date**: Date only field for acquisition tracking
    - **Last Inventory Date**: Date only field for audit trail maintenance
    - **Quantity**: New float field for bulk asset management
    - **Unit Cost**: New currency field for financial tracking and reporting
    - **Notes**: New multi-line text field for additional asset information
