## Nov-11, 2025

This release adds five new choice fields to the Core data model to improve operational tracking, engagement classification, and certification management.

-   **Engagement Mode**
    - New choice field with values: In Person, Virtual
    - Supports tracking how engagements and interactions are conducted.

-   **Lifecycle Stage**
    - New choice field with values: Planned, Development, Operational, Maintenance, Retired
    - Enables tracking of project, system, or process lifecycle stages for better resource planning and management.

-   **Visibility**
    - New choice field with values: Private, Team, Organizational, Public
    - Provides classification levels for information sharing and access control.

-   **Direction**
    - New choice field with values: Inbound, Outbound
    - Supports directional classification for communications, requests, or data flows.

-   **Simple Certification Status**
    - New choice field with values: Pending, Certified
    - Enables basic certification tracking for items requiring approval or validation.
